Second Floor (Collaborative Workspace): Open-Plan Environment: Designed to facilitate teamwork and creativity with flexible workspaces and collaborative zones. Meeting Spaces: Includes state-of-the-art meeting rooms and breakout areas to support brainstorming sessions and project discussions.

Third Floor (Executive Offices and Operations): Strategic Operations: Houses executive offices and administrative spaces for strategic decision-making and operational efficiency. Innovation Hub: Dedicated areas for research and development, fostering innovation and continuous improvement across departments.
